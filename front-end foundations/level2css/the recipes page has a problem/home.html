Magic Cake

Featured Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.

We also need to stress that whatever happedns to you as a result of consuming Magic Cake is you responsibility. You've been warned, and you can view our legel page for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a larrge bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy!
